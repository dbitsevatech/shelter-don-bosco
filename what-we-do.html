What We Do

  • What We Do

Art Work

Most arts and craft projects involve moving the hands and fingers. These movements involve fine motor skills and improve the overall muscle strength and control over movements. When a child colours or does a craft project, his motor skills gradually increase. Arts and crafts increase agility in kids and with practice manual dexterity also improve over time.

Adventure Camping

​Besides these, the boys learn scouting and even represented Shelter Don Bosco at the 13th National Boscoree event held at Nashik in December2018.

Awareness Programmes

Shelter makes an attempt to make kids aware by conducting various workshops, Quizzes, Sessions, Games, Movies and therapies on different topics related to Medical, General knowledge, English, Science and technology, various health issues and diseases, Drug De-addiction, Rehabilitation, Careers , savings, Education etc. Innovative and creative ways of activities are conducted in order to bring in active participation by the boys.

Careers

Shelter Don Bosco Facilitates children with Career Counseling, Udaan- A National Career Mela, and A Career Expo for Marginalized Youth (Annual event), Chota Udaan(monthly) and have now launched a Career Market Place called Naukriadda- a platform where children from various ngos can acess the webpage for choosing career options suitable to their profile.

Computer Education

With Education as a tool to build up a Child’s Career. Computer education has been given outmost importance. Shelter provides children with Computers to practice their Basic computers taught in school. English Speaking Tutorial Software are used to help them learn Spoken English. Children are given access to the Computer Class during their leisure time.

Cooking

A kitchen is a learning lab for children that can involve all of their senses. While kneading, tossing, pouring, smelling, cutting, and feeling foods they have fun and learn without being aware of it. Cooking with children at Shelter promotes the lifetime skill of healthy cooking starting at a young age.Cooking can aid children in acceptance of responsibility. Each child is given a task to contribute to the meal preparation and cleanup.

Counselling

Counselling is one of the major tools utilized for helping the marginalized youth and children. The life on the streets takes a huge emotional trauma on them. In order to help them cope with their feelings, they are supported through individual and group counselling.Various therapies and counselling tests are conducted at every stage i.e Outreach, Rehabilitation centre, Drop-in centre, Children’s Home and After Care home.

Excursions

Don Bosco believed much in outings and excursions which are essential for holistic education. And so Shelter Don Bosco with the much appreciated help of benefactors organizes excursions for the boys to heritage sites.

Health and Nutrition

​The Health and hygiene upkeep of our boys are looked after through medical camps that are conducted in the campus itself in collaboration with hospitals.

Music

Music plays an important role in our culture. You'll find music present in many aspects of our lives -Theater, television, movies, worship, holidays, celebrations - a natural part of our everyday experiences. Building on this music lessons at Shelter helps impacting child development, improve social skills, and benefits children in many aspects.

Savings

Shelter Don Bosco encourages boys to accumulate money for their future prospects. The concept of savings help shelter boys understand the need of saving and utilization of their money at the right point of time in their lives. Shelter facilitates children with a Bank account, Pass book, Pan Card and Fino Card.

Summer Camps

Summer camps are opportunities for children to spend their summer learning through recreation. Various activities, games, outings, training programs, awareness programs, picnics and outdoor camps are conducted During the 3 month Camp, Children are given various opportunities to explore their talent, learn leadership skills and team spirit. Children are divided in teams and fight for points to reach to the top and win the Summer Camp winning team title.

Sports

Sports is an integral part of the Salesians training and so the boys have coaching in Football and Hockey every morning during weekdays.
